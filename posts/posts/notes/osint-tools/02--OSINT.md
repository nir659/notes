## OSINT

- [Censys Search](https://search.censys.io/): Internet-wide scanning engine for discovering devices and services on the public internet.
- [Chaos by Projectdiscovery](https://chaos.projectdiscovery.io/): Real-time internet data platform providing dynamic datasets for reconnaissance and vulnerability assessments.
- [crt.sh | Certificate Search](https://crt.sh/): Free certificate transparency log search for identifying issued SSL certificates.
- [ViewDNS.info - Domain & IP Intelligence](https://viewdns.info/): Collection of DNS and network tools useful for domain and IP investigations.
- [Netlas](https://app.netlas.io/host/): Network intelligence platform for asset discovery and monitoring.
- [GreyNoise](https://viz.greynoise.io/ip/1.1.1.1): Internet-wide scan data for classifying IP addresses and noise filtering.
- [OSINT Framework](https://osintframework.com/): Curated directory of OSINT resources organized by investigation categories.
- [Malfrat's OSINT Map](https://map.malfrats.industries/): Visual OSINT tool showing hacking groups and threat activities geographically.
- [IntelTechniques Search Tool](https://inteltechniques.com/tools/): Suite of tools for digital investigations, including people search and social media lookup.
- [OSINT Earth](https://osintearth.com/search): Platform providing access to multiple OSINT data sources for investigations.
- [Epieos OSINT Tool](https://epieos.com/): OSINT platform focused on social media and dark web monitoring.
- [OSINT Industries](https://app.osint.industries/): Investigation platform offering real-time data with geospatial and timeline visualizations.
